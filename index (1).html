The Platform

Three components.
One source of truth.

ARIEL synthesizes government APIs, road cameras, weather data, and fire tracking into a single platform that translates raw conditions into immediate go/no-go decisions.

Web Platform
🖥️

Real-Time Logistics Dashboard

Live 6-camera grid across all major passes. Proprietary Pass Status Score (Green / Yellow / Red) combining road conditions, snowfall rates, and historical closure patterns — plus embedded fire, outage, and visibility feeds.

Community
📡

Community Intelligence Network

A verified Discord observer network of 30+ regular I-5 travelers — truckers, commuters, first responders — providing real-time field photos and ground-truth that no government API can replicate.

Media
🎙️

ARIEL Daily Logistics Brief

A 3–5 minute morning briefing at 7 AM covering pass conditions, fire status, and the 24-hour outlook. Free via Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and Google Podcasts — building ARIEL as the region's trusted emergency voice.

Signature Feature

The Pass Status Score

Our proprietary Green / Yellow / Red rating converts meteorological data and community reports into an immediate go/no-go decision. No interpretation required — one glance tells drivers whether to roll or hold.
